Our Story

Ms. Jhomarie Puente's (business owner) journey from aspiring entrepreneur to successful business owner showcases the power of determination and perseverance. Franchising a BigBrew Milktea shop, despite challenges like location selection, she turned her dream into reality. Today, her shop stands as an inspiration to others, proving that with passion and resilience, any dream is achievable.
